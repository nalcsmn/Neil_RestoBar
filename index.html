Gordon James Ramsay

Gordon James Ramsay OBE is a British chef, restaurateur, television personality, and writer. Born in Johnstone, Scotland, and raised in Stratford-upon-Avon, England, he founded his global restaurant group, Gordon Ramsay Restaurants, in 1997.

Pablo Logro

Pablo Logro, also known as Boy Logro or Chef Boy, is a Filipino celebrity chef known for his cooking shows, Idol sa Kusina and Chef Boy Logro: Kusina Master.

David Chang

David Chang is an American restaurateur, author, and television personality. He is the founder of the Momofuku restaurant group, which includes Momofuku Noodle Bar, Momofuku Ssäm Bar, Milk Bar
